## 🎯 Overview

Dream60 is a competitive auction platform where players participate in **hourly auctions** to win cash prizes. Each auction runs for multiple rounds, and players must strategically bid while managing their budgets to become one of the top 3 winners.

### Key Features:
- ⏰ **Hourly Auctions** - New auctions every hour
- 💰 **Multiple Prize Tiers** - Top 3 winners receive prizes
- 🎯 **Strategic Bidding** - Up to 4 rounds of competitive bidding
- 📊 **Real-time Updates** - Live auction status and participant tracking
- 🏆 **Prize Claiming** - Winners claim prizes via UPI within 30 minutes
- 📜 **Complete History** - Track all your past auctions and winnings

---

## 🎮 How to Play

### Step 1: Join an Auction
1. Browse available hourly auctions in the dashboard
2. Check the **prize value** and **entry fee** for each auction
3. Click **"Join Auction"** to participate
4. Pay the entry fee to secure your spot

### Step 2: Participate in Rounds
- Each auction has **up to 4 rounds** of bidding
- Place your bids strategically in each round
- Monitor your **total spent** vs **remaining balance**
- Avoid elimination by staying competitive and active

### Step 3: Compete to Win
- Survive all rounds to qualify for prizes
- Top 3 players with best performance win
- Winners are ranked **1st, 2nd, and 3rd place**

### Step 4: Claim Your Prize
- If you win, claim your prize within **30 minutes**
- Navigate to **Auction History** page
- Find the auction you won and click **"Claim Prize"**
- Enter your **UPI ID** for prize transfer
- Pay the remaining fee based on your rank
- Receive your winnings!

## 📋 Game Rules

### Eligibility
- ✅ Must register an account to participate
- ✅ Must have sufficient balance for entry fee and bidding
- ✅ Can join multiple auctions simultaneously

### Entry Requirements
- 💵 **Entry Fee**: Varies per auction (displayed on auction card)
- 👥 **Minimum Players**: Auctions need minimum participants to start
- ⏰ **Join Before Start**: Must join before the auction's scheduled time slot

### Bidding Rules
- 🎯 Each round allows multiple bids per player
- 💰 Bids deduct from your available balance
- ⚠️ Cannot bid if insufficient funds
- 📊 All bids are tracked and recorded in real-time
- 🔄 Strategic bidding improves your chances of winning

### Elimination System
- ❌ **Low Activity**: Players who don't participate in rounds get eliminated
- ❌ **Budget Exceeded**: Players who overspend may be eliminated
- ❌ **Strategic Elimination**: Lowest performers in each round may be eliminated
- ✅ **Qualified Players**: Must survive all rounds without elimination to win

## 🔄 Auction Rounds

Each auction consists of **up to 4 competitive rounds**:

### Round 1: Opening Round
- All participants place initial bids
- Sets the competitive pace for the auction
- First elimination checkpoint
- Inactive players removed

### Round 2: Mid Game
- Competition intensifies among active players
- Strategic bidding becomes crucial
- More eliminations may occur
- Budget management tested

### Round 3: Advanced Round
- Only serious competitors remain
- Budget management becomes critical
- High-stakes bidding phase
- Qualification window narrows

### Round 4: Final Round
- Last chance to secure top position
- Determines final rankings for prizes
- Winners are selected after this round completes
- Most competitive phase of the auction

### Early Completion
Auction may end **before Round 4** if:
- ✅ Only **3 or fewer qualified players** remain after any round
- ✅ All other players have been eliminated
- ✅ Winners are automatically determined from remaining players
- ✅ Auction transitions to completion phase immediately

## 🏆 Winner Selection

### How Winners Are Determined

After all rounds complete (or early completion criteria met), the top 3 performers are selected as winners based on:
- 📊 **Total Participation**: Active participation across all rounds
- 💰 **Strategic Bidding**: Quality and quantity of bids placed
- 🎯 **Budget Management**: Efficient use of available funds
- ✅ **Survival**: Not eliminated during any round
- 📈 **Performance Score**: Calculated from multiple factors

### Winner Rankings
1. 🥇 **1st Place** - Best overall performance and highest score
2. 🥈 **2nd Place** - Second best performance among participants
3. 🥉 **3rd Place** - Third best performance among participants

### Automatic Winner Marking
- Winners are automatically marked in the database when auction completes
- All participants' data is saved to **Auction History** collection
- Prize claim status is set to **"PENDING"** for all winners
- 30-minute countdown timer starts immediately for prize claiming

## 🎁 Prize Claiming

### Claim Process (30-Minute Window)

Winners have **exactly 30 minutes** after auction completion to claim their prize:

#### Step 1: Automatic Notification
- ✅ Winners are marked immediately when auction completes
- ✅ Prize claim status set to **"PENDING"**
- ⏰ **30-minute countdown** begins automatically
- 📧 Check your auction history for claim eligibility

#### Step 2: Navigate to Auction History
- 📜 Go to **"Auction History"** page from the navigation menu
- 🏆 Find the auction you won (marked with winner badge)
- 🎯 Look for the **"Claim Prize"** card at the top of the page
- ⏱️ View countdown timer showing remaining claim time

#### Step 3: Submit Claim Details
- 💳 Enter your **UPI ID** (e.g., username@paytm, user@googlepay)
- 📝 Optionally add **Payment Reference** for remaining fee verification
- ✅ Click **"Submit Claim"** button to process
- 🔄 System validates your claim request

#### Step 4: Confirmation
- ✅ Prize status updates to **"CLAIMED"** upon successful submission
- 💰 Prize transfer initiated to your provided UPI ID
- 📧 Confirmation notification displayed
- 📊 Auction history updated with claim timestamp

### Important Prize Claiming Rules:
- ⏰ **Must claim within 30 minutes** from auction completion
- ⚠️ **Expired prizes** cannot be claimed after the deadline
- 💯 **Valid UPI ID required** for prize transfer processing
- 📱 **Accurate payment details** ensure smooth prize delivery
- 🏆 **All ranks (1, 2, 3)** can claim prizes, not just rank 1
- 💳 **Remaining fee** must be paid based on your winner rank

### Prize Claim Status:
- 🟡 **PENDING**: Prize available for claiming (within 30 minutes)
- 🟢 **CLAIMED**: Prize successfully claimed by winner
- 🔴 **EXPIRED**: 30-minute window passed, prize forfeited

## 📜 Auction History

### View Your Complete History

Access comprehensive records of all your auction participations through the Auction History page:

### What's Included in History:
- 🎫 **Auction Details**: Unique ID, auction name, prize value, time slot, date
- 💰 **Financial Summary**: Entry fee paid, total amount bid, total amount spent
- 📊 **Performance Stats**: Rounds participated, total bids placed, bid patterns
- ❌ **Elimination Status**: Whether eliminated and in which specific round
- 🏆 **Winner Information**: Winner status, rank (1/2/3), and prize amount
- 🎁 **Prize Claim Status**: Current status (PENDING/CLAIMED/EXPIRED)
- ⏱️ **Claim Deadline**: Countdown timer for active prize claims
- 📈 **Performance Metrics**: Success rate, bidding efficiency

### Access Your History:
1. Navigate to **"Auction History"** from the main navigation menu
2. View all your past auctions sorted by date (most recent first)
3. Click on any auction card to see detailed statistics
4. For won auctions, see **claim prize card** with countdown timer
5. Filter by status: All, Won, Lost, Active Claims

### History Statistics Dashboard:
- 📈 **Total Auctions**: Number of auctions you've participated in
- 🏆 **Total Wins**: Number of times you won (any rank: 1st, 2nd, or 3rd)
- 💰 **Total Spent**: Cumulative amount spent across all auctions
- 💵 **Total Won**: Cumulative prize money won from all victories
- 📊 **Win Rate**: Percentage of auctions won vs participated
- 💹 **Net Gain/Loss**: Total won minus total spent (profitability)
- 🎯 **Best Rank**: Highest rank achieved (1st place is best)

### Automatic History Recording:
The system automatically saves complete auction data when auctions complete:
- ✅ All participants recorded in **AuctionHistory** database collection
- ✅ Winner information and rankings saved
- ✅ Prize claim deadlines calculated and stored
- ✅ Performance metrics and bidding data preserved
- ✅ Searchable by user ID for personalized history

## 🔄 Development Setup

### Prerequisites
- Node.js 18+ installed
- MongoDB instance running
- npm or bun package manager

### Installation
```bash
# Install dependencies
npm install
# or
bun install

# Set up environment variables
cp .env.example .env

# Start development server
npm run dev
# or
bun run dev
```

### Environment Variables
Create a `.env` file with:
```
MONGODB_URI=your_mongodb_connection_string
PORT=5000
NODE_ENV=development
```
